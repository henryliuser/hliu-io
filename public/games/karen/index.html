Karen-tastrophe - GMTK 2020 Theme: "Out of Control"

Programming, Game Design: Henry Liu, Nidhir Guggilla
Art, Graphics Design: Kethan Sivarapu, Regina Yu

Welcome to your store.#

After 5 long years of work, you were finally promoted to regional manager. Unluckily for you, your store seems to be starting to attract Karens… and they’re OUT OF CONTROL!

Succeed by eliminating all the Karens before they eat your staff and customers. Featuring vaccines, essential oils, toilet paper, and globes as usable items!
